BRAVE TIGERS

Hidden deep within the Sumatran rainforests, a special breed of Tigers have been in hibernation. Years ago they retreated to the depths of the jungle, away from the droves of humans that were leading them towards extinction. It was here they waited patiently, eager to have their vengeance, but aware that the odds were not in their favour.
They are the Brave Tigers.
The surge of the Metaverse has awoken them from a lengthy sleep, and they've seen a glowing opportunity. As the humans were busying themselves with their own destruction, the Brave Tigers were training, arming themselves and learning the intricacies of warfare. With that, they've given themselves an advantage, and grown a force to be reckoned with and reversed the effects of extinction on their kind.
For years the humans have ruled Earth, with practised methods that the Tigers couldn't match. But the metaverse is fresh ground, untouched territory, the perfect chance for the Brave Tigers to bring a fair fight. They'll take back the virtual jungles, and conquer the digital space.
10,000 Brave Tigers are ready to take the fight to the humans, and they're not to be underestimated.
